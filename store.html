Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2: Trailer, Release Date, Plot & News to Know

While anime fans are just beginning to enjoy the Spring 2023 season,
many are already looking forward to Summer and Season 2 of the dark
shonen juggernaut Jujutsu Kaisen. After the booming success of Season 1
and the prequel film Jujutsu Kaisen 0, glimpses of Season 2 have been
revealed with stunning animation and more layers to the story that are
sure to amaze. Similar to the prequel movie, JJK's second season will be
another blast to the past with the focus on Satoru Gojo. Though fans
quickly grew to love Season 1's plot and characters like Yuji Itadori,
Megumi Fushiguro, Nobara Kugisaki and their classmates at Jujutsu High,
the upcoming "Gojo's Past" arc is important in shaping the overall story
in a truly original way.

The Plot and Trailer for Jujutsu
Kaisen Season 2

Season 2's plot will take place in 2006 and is centered around
second-year student Gojo and his mission alongside his best friend
Suguru Geto. As Jujutsu High students, they are tasked with the job of
escorting and protecting a young girl named Riko Amanai, who is to be
used as a Vessel for the figure known as Master Tengen. Glimpses of
younger Gojo's relationships with Geto, Riko and other classmates are
seen, as is a more vibrant energy from the younger, possibly more naive
version of the present-day strongest sorcerer.

When the Jujutsu Kaisen anime's official Twitter channel released the
full Season 2 trailer on March 24th, and then by TOHO animation's
YouTube channel on March 25, fans were set ablaze with further details
on how the story is shaping up. Going further into Gojo's student days,
the trailer focuses on scenes with him and Geto while including hints of
other recognizable characters from Season 1. Younger versions of Shouko
Ieiri, Jujutsu High's Doctor, and possibly Utahime Iori, the Kyoto
campus teacher, are briefly shown. Fans might have overlooked their
presence in the trailer thanks to the surprise introduction of one Toji
Fushiguro.

Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2 Release Date
and Production Updates

It's already clear that Season 2 of Jujutsu Kaisen will provide more of
Studio MAPPA's jaw-dropping animation, with many fans comparing the
trailer's visuals and vibe to a high-budget film. MAPPA CEO Manabu
Otsuka shared his reflections on the production, stating: “We are not
going to be doing the same thing and repeat ourselves. To the fans of
the series, I would like to show even greater success and bigger growth
in season 2.”

As for the voice actors, all those from Season 1 of Jujutsu Kaisen are
reprising their roles in Season 2. Takahiro Sakurai will be voicing as
Geto once more, while the voices of Riko Amanai and Toji Fushiguro will
be played by Anna Nagase (Yurei Deco) and Takehito Koyasu (Gintama)
respectively. A new director is in charge, with Shōta Goshozono (Ranking
of Kings) replacing Sung Hoo Park.

Jujutsu Kaisen Season 2 is set to premiere on July 6th, 2023. It will be
broadcast on television in Japan and available for streaming online via
Crunchyroll. For newcomers to the franchise and longtime fans wanting to
get caught up, Jujutsu Kaisen Season 1 and the film Jujutsu Kaisen 0 are
both available to watch on Crunchyroll right now.
